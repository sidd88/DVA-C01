### On demand

/di'mɑ:nd/: đòi hỏi, yêu cầu.

> With the AWS, you are able to have
services and servers that can be used `on demand`
and scaled very easily.

### Revolution

/revə'lu:ʃn/: cuộc cách mạng

> So AWS has `revolutionized` IT over time.
